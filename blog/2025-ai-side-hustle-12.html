12 条 AI 副业路径 12 AI Side Hustle Paths

1

AI 写作变现 AI-Powered Writing Monetization

做什么：用 AI 辅助写作，在内容平台赚取稿费和流量分成。包括公众号长文、小红书种草文、知乎回答、头条号文章等。AI 负责初稿，你负责选题、审核和优化。工具：ChatGPT / Claude 做初稿，Notion AI 做润色，Grammarly 做英文校对。怎么开始：选一个你熟悉的领域，每天用 AI 写 2-3 篇内容，坚持发布一个月。预期收入：1,000-8,000 元/月，头部作者可达 20,000+。 What: Use AI-assisted writing to earn fees and traffic revenue on content platforms. Including WeChat Official Account articles, Xiaohongshu posts, Zhihu answers, Toutiao articles, etc. AI handles first drafts, you handle topic selection, review, and optimization. Tools: ChatGPT/Claude for first drafts, Notion AI for polishing, Grammarly for English proofreading. How to start: Pick a domain you know well, write 2-3 pieces daily with AI, publish consistently for one month. Expected income: $150-$1,200/month, top creators can reach $3,000+.

2

AI 设计接单 AI Design Freelancing

做什么：用 AI 生成高质量设计素材，在闲鱼、淘宝、Fiverr 等平台接设计单。包括 Logo 设计、海报设计、电商主图、PPT 美化、社交媒体配图等。工具：Midjourney / DALL-E 3 生成图像，Canva 做排版，Figma 做精细调整。怎么开始：先用 AI 做 10 个不同风格的设计作品当作品集，然后挂到接单平台。预期收入：2,000-15,000 元/月，熟练后单张海报可收 200-500 元。 What: Generate high-quality design assets with AI, take freelance orders on platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, etc. Including logo design, posters, e-commerce images, PPT beautification, social media graphics. Tools: Midjourney/DALL-E 3 for image generation, Canva for layout, Figma for fine-tuning. How to start: First create 10 designs in different styles as your portfolio, then list on freelance platforms. Expected income: $300-$2,200/month, experienced freelancers can charge $30-$70 per poster.

3

AI 编程做 SaaS AI-Powered SaaS Building

做什么：用 AI 编程工具（如 Cursor）构建小型 SaaS 产品并收取月费。适合做垂直领域的小工具：比如简历生成器、发票工具、社交媒体排期工具等。工具：Cursor / Windsurf 写代码，Vercel / Railway 部署，Stripe / LemonSqueezy 收款。怎么开始：找一个你自己的痛点，用 Cursor 在一周内做出 MVP，上线后找第一批用户。预期收入：月收入 0-50,000 元不等，大多数产品 0-3,000 元，少数爆款可达 10,000+。 What: Build small SaaS products using AI coding tools (like Cursor) and charge monthly subscriptions. Best for vertical niche tools: resume generators, invoicing tools, social media schedulers, etc. Tools: Cursor/Windsurf for coding, Vercel/Railway for deployment, Stripe/LemonSqueezy for payments. How to start: Find a pain point of your own, build an MVP with Cursor in one week, find first users after launch. Expected income: $0-$7,500/month, most products earn $0-$450,少数爆款可达 $1,500+.

4

AI 视频制作 AI Video Production

做什么：用 AI 工具批量制作短视频内容，在抖音、B 站、YouTube 等平台发布赚取广告分成和带货收入。适合做知识科普、书评、产品解读等类型。工具：ChatGPT 写脚本，ElevenLabs / 剪映配音，Runway / Pika 生成视频片段，CapCut 剪辑。怎么开始：选一个细分领域，用 AI 批量生产视频，日更或隔日更，坚持 3 个月。预期收入：1,000-30,000 元/月，头部创作者可达 100,000+。 What: Batch-produce short video content with AI tools, publish on YouTube, TikTok, etc. to earn ad revenue and affiliate income. Best for knowledge explainer, book reviews, product analysis formats. Tools: ChatGPT for scripts, ElevenLabs/CapCut for voiceover, Runway/Pika for video clips, CapCut for editing. How to start: Pick a niche, batch-produce videos with AI, post daily or every other day, persist for 3 months. Expected income: $150-$4,500/month, top creators can reach $15,000+.

AI 翻译服务 AI Translation Services

做什么：用 AI 做中英/中日等语种的翻译工作，承接企业翻译、文档翻译、网站本地化等订单。AI 翻译质量已经接近甚至超越普通翻译水平，但价格远低于人工翻译。工具：DeepL / GPT-4 做初翻，你做审校和术语统一。怎么开始：在翻译平台（如有道翻译、ProZ）注册，先接小单积累评价。预期收入：3,000-15,000 元/月，专业领域（法律、医学）更高。 What: Use AI for translation work across Chinese-English, Chinese-Japanese, etc., taking corporate translation, document translation, website localization orders. AI translation quality now approaches or exceeds average human translators, at a fraction of the cost. Tools: DeepL/GPT-4 for initial translation, you handle review and terminology consistency. How to start: Register on translation platforms (ProZ, Gengo), take small orders first to build reviews. Expected income: $450-$2,200/month, higher for specialized fields (legal, medical).

AI 教学/咨询 AI Teaching & Consulting

做什么：教别人使用 AI 工具，或帮企业做 AI 落地咨询。包括一对一教学、企业内训、AI 工作流搭建等。市场上大量企业想用 AI 但不知道怎么用，这就是你的机会。工具：腾讯会议 / Zoom 做远程教学，Notion 做课件整理，各种 AI 工具本身。怎么开始：先免费帮 3-5 个朋友或小企业做 AI 咨询，积累案例和口碑。预期收入：5,000-50,000 元/月，企业咨询项目可达数万元。 What: Teach others to use AI tools, or consult enterprises on AI adoption. Including 1-on-1 coaching, corporate training, AI workflow setup. Many companies want to use AI but don't know how — that's your opportunity. Tools: Zoom/Google Meet for remote sessions, Notion for materials, various AI tools themselves. How to start: Free AI consulting for 3-5 friends or small businesses first, build cases and word-of-mouth. Expected income: $750-$7,500/month, enterprise consulting projects can reach several thousand dollars each.

做什么：用 AI 生成电商产品展示图、模特图、场景图。很多淘宝/亚马逊卖家需要大量商品图片，传统拍摄成本高，AI 生成成本低且速度快。工具：Midjourney / Stable Diffusion 生成图片，Photoshop / Canva 做后期处理。怎么开始：找几个做电商的朋友，免费帮他们做一批图片，拿效果说话。预期收入：2,000-10,000 元/月，批量接单后可达 20,000+。 What: Generate e-commerce product display images, model shots, and scene images with AI. Many Amazon/Shopify sellers need large volumes of product images — traditional photography is expensive, AI generation is cheap and fast. Tools: Midjourney/Stable Diffusion for image generation, Photoshop/Canva for post-processing. How to start: Find friends doing e-commerce, generate a batch of images for free, let results speak. Expected income: $300-$1,500/month, up to $3,000+ with bulk orders.
