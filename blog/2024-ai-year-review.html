2024 AI 年度总结：从百模大战到落地为王 2024 AI Year in Review: From Model Wars to Real-World Impact

2024.12.28 — 回顾这一年，AI 不再是科技圈的专属话题，而是真正走进了普通人的生活Looking back at a year when AI moved beyond tech circles and into everyday life

2024，AI 的"成人礼" 2024: AI's Coming of Age

如果用一句话概括 2024 年的 AI 行业，那就是：从"能不能"到"好不好"的转变。2023 年是 AI 的"元年"——ChatGPT 横空出世，所有人都在惊叹"AI 竟然能做到这样"。而 2024 年，大家不再满足于演示 Demo，而是开始追问"这个东西到底能不能帮我解决实际问题"。 If there's one sentence to sum up the AI industry in 2024, it's this: the shift from "can it?" to "is it good enough?" 2023 was AI's "year zero" — ChatGPT burst onto the scene and everyone marveled at "what AI can do." In 2024, people were no longer satisfied with demos. Instead, they started asking "can this thing actually solve my real problems?"

这一年，AI 模型从实验室走向了千家万户。从写代码到做视频，从聊天助手到编程工具，AI 开始在具体场景中创造真实价值。对于普通人来说，2024 年可能是第一次真正感受到"AI 和我有关系"的一年。 This year, AI models moved from the lab into millions of homes. From writing code to generating videos, from chat assistants to programming tools, AI started creating real value in specific scenarios. For ordinary people, 2024 might be the first year they truly felt "AI has something to do with me."

"2024 年最重要的 AI 趋势不是某个模型变聪明了，而是 AI 终于开始变得'有用'了。" "The most important AI trend of 2024 wasn't that some model got smarter — it's that AI finally started becoming 'useful.'"

六大事件，定义了这一年的 AI Six Events That Defined AI in 2024

让我们用时间线的方式，回顾 2024 年最值得记住的 AI 大事件。每一个事件都深刻改变了 AI 的走向，也深刻改变了我们使用 AI 的方式。 Let's walk through 2024's most memorable AI events on a timeline. Each one profoundly changed the trajectory of AI, and how we use it.

1

GPT-4o 发布：多模态时代全面到来 GPT-4o Launch: The Full Multimodal Era Arrives

2024 年 5 月，OpenAI 发布了 GPT-4o。"o" 代表 "omni"——全能。这个模型最大的突破在于它真正实现了文本、语音、图像的统一理解和生成。你可以对着它说话，它能实时看到你的屏幕、听到你的语气、理解你的表情。更重要的是，它的速度极快，延迟几乎和真人对话一样。对于普通人来说，这意味着和 AI 交互不再只是"打字聊天"，而是真正可以"说话交流"。 In May 2024, OpenAI released GPT-4o. The "o" stands for "omni" — all-capable. The biggest breakthrough was truly unified understanding and generation of text, voice, and images. You could speak to it, and it could see your screen in real time, hear your tone, and understand your expressions. More importantly, it was incredibly fast, with latency nearly matching human conversation. For ordinary people, this meant interacting with AI was no longer just "typing in a chat" but truly "having a conversation."

2

Sora 震撼登场：AI 视频生成进入新纪元 Sora Shocks the World: A New Era of AI Video Generation

2024 年初，OpenAI 展示了 Sora——一个能根据文字描述生成长达 60 秒高质量视频的模型。Sora 的视频质量令人震惊：物理规律准确、光影真实、镜头运动流畅。虽然它直到年底才正式面向公众开放，但 Sora 的出现让整个内容创作行业都坐不住了。电影制作人、广告公司、自媒体博主都开始认真思考一个问题：未来的视频内容，有多少会是 AI 生成的？ In early 2024, OpenAI unveiled Sora — a model capable of generating up to 60 seconds of high-quality video from text descriptions. Sora's video quality was stunning: physically accurate, photorealistic lighting, smooth camera movements. Although it wasn't publicly available until the end of the year, Sora's debut made the entire content creation industry sit up. Filmmakers, advertising agencies, and content creators all started seriously considering one question: how much future video content will be AI-generated?

3

Cursor 崛起：AI 编程工具的真正王者 Cursor Rises: The True King of AI Coding Tools

如果说 2023 年的 AI 编程还停留在"让 ChatGPT 帮你写代码片段"的阶段，那么 2024 年 Cursor 的崛起彻底改变了游戏规则。Cursor 不只是一个聊天窗口——它是一个真正理解你整个项目的 AI 程序员。它的 Composer 功能可以同时修改多个文件，Tab 补全可以预测你下一行要写什么，Agent 模式甚至可以自主执行复杂任务。到 2024 年底，Cursor 已经成为开发者圈子里最不可或缺的工具之一，甚至很多不会写代码的人也在用它做产品。 If AI coding in 2023 was still stuck at the "let ChatGPT write code snippets for you" stage, then Cursor's rise in 2024 completely changed the game. Cursor isn't just a chat window — it's an AI programmer that truly understands your entire project. Its Composer feature can modify multiple files simultaneously, Tab completion predicts your next line, and Agent mode can autonomously execute complex tasks. By the end of 2024, Cursor had become one of the most indispensable tools in the developer community — even people who can't code are using it to build products.

4

国产模型井喷：中国 AI 力量的集体爆发 Chinese AI Models Explode: A Collective Surge of China's AI Power

2024 年是中国 AI 模型"井喷"的一年。阿里的通义千问、字节跳动的豆包、月之暗面的 Kimi、百度的文心一言、智谱的 GLM……各家大模型你追我赶，性能不断提升。更重要的是，很多国产模型在中文理解、文化适配方面已经不输甚至超越了国外模型。对于中国用户来说，这意味着你可以用更低的成本、更流畅的体验使用 AI。国产 AI 工具的免费策略也大大降低了普通人接触 AI 的门槛。 2024 was a year of explosive growth for Chinese AI models. Alibaba's Tongyi Qianwen, ByteDance's Doubao, Moonshot AI's Kimi, Baidu's ERNIE Bot, Zhipu's GLM — every major model raced forward with continuously improving performance. More importantly, many Chinese models now match or even surpass foreign models in Chinese language understanding and cultural adaptation. For Chinese users, this means you can use AI at lower costs with a smoother experience. The free-tier strategies of domestic AI tools have also significantly lowered the barrier for ordinary people to access AI.

5

AI 价格战：使用成本断崖式下降 AI Price Wars: Costs Plummet Off a Cliff

2024 年的另一个关键词是"便宜"。从 OpenAI 到 Google 到国内的各大厂商，API 调用价格一路狂跌。GPT-4 的价格相比发布时下降了超过 90%，很多国产模型更是直接免费开放使用。这对普通人意味着什么？意味着你不需要花很多钱，就能用上最好的 AI。AI 不再是科技公司的奢侈品，而是变成了像水电一样的基础设施。 Another keyword for 2024 was "cheap." From OpenAI to Google to every major domestic vendor, API prices plummeted. GPT-4's price dropped over 90% compared to launch, and many Chinese models went completely free. What does this mean for ordinary people? It means you don't need to spend a fortune to access the best AI. AI is no longer a luxury for tech companies — it's become infrastructure, like water and electricity.

6

Agent 元年：AI 从"工具"进化为"员工" Year of the Agent: AI Evolves from "Tool" to "Employee"

2024 年下半年，"AI Agent"成为最火的行业概念。Agent 不再是那种你问一句它答一句的被动工具——它可以自主规划任务、调用工具、执行多步骤操作。Cursor 的 Agent 模式可以自主完成一个功能开发；各种 AI 助手可以自动帮你整理邮件、安排日程、处理数据。Agent 的本质是让 AI 从"等指令"变成"主动干活"。虽然 2024 年的 Agent 还不够完美，但它预示了一个重要方向：未来，每个人都可以拥有自己的 AI 助手团队。 In the second half of 2024, "AI Agent" became the hottest industry concept. Agents are no longer passive tools that answer one question at a time — they can autonomously plan tasks, call tools, and execute multi-step operations. Cursor's Agent mode can autonomously complete a feature development; various AI assistants can automatically organize your email, schedule your calendar, and process data. The essence of Agent is transforming AI from "waiting for instructions" to "proactively working." Although 2024's Agents aren't perfect yet, they signal an important direction: in the future, everyone could have their own AI assistant team.

对普通人来说，2024 意味着什么？ What Does 2024 Mean for Ordinary People?

回顾这些大事件，你会发现一个共同点：AI 正在从"极客的玩具"变成"普通人的工具"。价格下降了，门槛降低了，使用场景丰富了。以前你可能觉得"AI 离我很远"，但现在： Looking back at these major events, you'll notice a common thread: AI is transforming from "geeks' toys" into "tools for everyone." Prices dropped, barriers lowered, use cases multiplied. You might have once felt "AI is far from me," but now:

你可以在豆包上免费和 AI 聊天，帮你写文案、总结会议纪要；你可以用 Cursor 不会写代码也能做出一个 App；你可以用 Kimi 帮你读长文档、整理资料；你可以用 Sora（虽然还没完全开放）的思路理解 AI 视频生成的未来。 You can chat with AI for free on Doubao to write copy and summarize meeting notes; you can use Cursor to build an app without knowing how to code; you can use Kimi to read long documents and organize materials; you can look at Sora's approach (even if not fully public yet) to understand the future of AI video generation.

关键转变 The Key Shift

2023 年，人们问的是"AI 是什么"。2024 年，人们开始问"AI 能帮我做什么"。这个转变看似微小，实则是整个行业从技术驱动转向应用驱动的标志。当消费者开始关心"怎么用"而不是"是什么"的时候，一项技术才真正走向成熟。 In 2023, people asked "What is AI?" In 2024, people started asking "What can AI do for me?" This shift may seem small, but it actually marks the entire industry's transition from technology-driven to application-driven. When consumers start caring about "how to use" rather than "what is it," a technology is truly maturing.

我的个人年度感悟 My Personal Reflections on the Year

作为一个在 2024 年深度使用各种 AI 工具的人，我有几点切身的感受想分享。 As someone who deeply used various AI tools throughout 2024, I have a few personal reflections to share.

第一，AI 工具的选择比想象中重要。不同的 AI 工具适合不同的场景。ChatGPT 适合通用对话和写作，Cursor 适合编程，Kimi 适合处理长文档，豆包适合日常问答。不要试图用一个大模型解决所有问题，找到最适合你当前需求的工具，效率会高得多。 First, choosing the right AI tool matters more than you think. Different AI tools suit different scenarios. ChatGPT is best for general conversation and writing, Cursor for programming, Kimi for processing long documents, Doubao for everyday Q&A. Don't try to use one model for everything — find the tool that best fits your current need and you'll be much more productive.

第二，学会"提问"比学会"使用工具"更重要。同样一个 AI 工具，有人用它产出垃圾，有人用它创造价值。区别不在于工具本身，而在于你如何向它表达需求。2024 年，"Prompt Engineering"（提示词工程）从一个小众技能变成了核心竞争力。 Second, learning to "ask" matters more than learning to "use tools." With the same AI tool, some people produce garbage while others create value. The difference isn't the tool itself — it's how you express your needs to it. In 2024, "Prompt Engineering" went from a niche skill to a core competency.

第三，不要等"准备好了"才开始用 AI。AI 工具迭代速度极快，你现在学的操作方式可能三个月后就变了。最好的学习方式就是现在就开始用，在实践中摸索，在错误中学习。 Third, don't wait until you're "ready" to start using AI. AI tools iterate incredibly fast — the way you use them now might be completely different in three months. The best way to learn is to start right now, explore through practice, and learn from mistakes.

展望 2025 Looking Ahead to 2025

站在 2024 年末，展望 2025，我认为有几个趋势值得关注： Standing at the end of 2024, looking ahead to 2025, I think several trends are worth watching:

AI Agent 将会更加成熟和实用，每个人都能拥有自己的 AI 助手团队；AI 编程工具会进一步降低门槛，真正实现"所想即所得"；AI 视频生成会变得更加普及，改变内容创作的方式；国产 AI 将在更多领域追上甚至超越国际水平。 AI Agents will become more mature and practical, and everyone will be able to have their own AI assistant team; AI coding tools will further lower barriers, truly achieving "what you think is what you get"; AI video generation will become more widespread, transforming content creation; Chinese AI will catch up to and even surpass international levels in more domains.

2024 年的 AI，关键词是"落地"。模型不再是实验室里的论文，而是变成了你手机里的 App、你电脑上的工具、你工作中的助手。2025 年，关键词将是"渗透"——AI 将更深地嵌入到我们生活的每一个角落。对于普通人来说，现在开始学习和使用 AI，正是最好的时机。 The keyword for AI in 2024 was "landing." Models were no longer papers in labs — they became apps on your phone, tools on your computer, assistants in your work. In 2025, the keyword will be "penetration" — AI will embed itself deeper into every corner of our lives. For ordinary people, there has never been a better time to start learning and using AI.
