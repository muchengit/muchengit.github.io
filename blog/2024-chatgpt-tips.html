ChatGPT 使用秘籍：10 个让输出质量翻倍的实战技巧 10 ChatGPT Tips to Double Your Output Quality

2024.09.08 — 掌握这些技巧，让你的每一次对话都获得高质量输出Master these techniques and get high-quality output from every conversation

为什么同样的 ChatGPT，别人用得好，你用得差？ Why Does the Same ChatGPT Work Better for Others Than for You?

很多人用 ChatGPT 的方式是这样的：打开对话框，随便打几个字，比如"帮我写个方案"，然后对 AI 的输出不满意，叹口气说"ChatGPT 也就这样吧"。 Many people use ChatGPT like this: open the chat, type a few random words like "write me a proposal," then feel unsatisfied with the AI's output and sigh, "ChatGPT is just so-so."

问题不在 ChatGPT，在于你如何和它对话。同样的 ChatGPT，不同的提问方式，输出质量可以差 10 倍。这篇文章整理了我长期使用 ChatGPT 总结出的 10 个实用技巧，每一个都能立竿见影地提升你的输出质量。 The problem isn't ChatGPT — it's how you talk to it. The same ChatGPT with different prompting approaches can produce output that differs in quality by 10x. This article compiles 10 practical techniques I've summarized from extensive ChatGPT use, each of which can immediately improve your output quality.

"ChatGPT 是一面镜子。你给它模糊的指令，它就给你模糊的输出；你给它精确的指令，它就给你精确的结果。" "ChatGPT is a mirror. Give it vague instructions, you get vague output; give it precise instructions, you get precise results."

10 个实战技巧 10 Practical Techniques

技巧 1：给 AI 一个明确的角色设定 Tip 1: Give AI a Clear Role

不要直接让 ChatGPT 帮你做事，先告诉它"你是谁"。角色设定会显著影响 AI 的回答风格、深度和专业性。一个没有角色设定的 ChatGPT，回答往往泛泛而谈；一个有角色设定的 ChatGPT，回答会更有针对性和专业度。 Don't just tell ChatGPT to do something — first tell it "who you are." Role setting significantly affects the AI's answer style, depth, and professionalism. A ChatGPT without a role gives generic answers; one with a role gives targeted, professional responses. 

你是一位有 10 年经验的资深产品经理，擅长做用户增长和商业化策略。
现在请你帮我分析一下，一个面向大学生的二手交易平台，应该如何设计冷启动策略？You are a senior product manager with 10 years of experience, specializing in user growth and monetization strategy.
Now please help me analyze how a second-hand trading platform targeting college students should design its cold-start strategy.

对比一下，如果你只问"帮我分析二手交易平台的冷启动策略"，得到的答案会笼统得多。有了角色设定，AI 会从产品经理的视角出发，给出更有实操价值的分析。 Compare this to just asking "help me analyze the cold-start strategy for a second-hand trading platform" — the answer would be much more generic. With a role setting, the AI answers from a product manager's perspective, giving much more actionable analysis.

技巧 2：提供示例，让 AI 照着做 Tip 2: Provide Examples for AI to Follow

如果你想得到特定风格的输出，最快的方式就是给它一个示例。AI 是极强的模仿者——给它看一个样本，它就能模仿样本的风格、结构、语调来生成内容。 If you want output in a specific style, the fastest way is to give it an example. AI is an incredibly strong imitator — show it a sample, and it can mimic the style, structure, and tone to generate content. 

请帮我写一封客户跟进邮件。风格参考以下示例：

---
Hi 张总，
上周我们讨论了关于新系统的部署方案，当时您提到希望在 10 月前完成上线。
经过内部评估，我们建议采用分阶段部署的策略：
- 第一阶段（10/1-10/15）：核心模块上线
- 第二阶段（10/16-10/31）：辅助模块上线
是否方便本周安排一个 30 分钟的电话，确认方案细节？

---

请参考这个风格，帮我写一封关于 XX 项目的跟进邮件。Please help me write a client follow-up email. Style reference:

---
Hi Zhang,
Last week we discussed the deployment plan for the new system, and you mentioned wanting to go live before October.
After internal evaluation, we recommend a phased deployment approach:
- Phase 1 (10/1-10/15): Core modules go live
- Phase 2 (10/16-10/31): Supporting modules go live
Would you be available for a 30-minute call this week to confirm the details?

---

Please reference this style and write a follow-up email for the XX project.

技巧 3：要求分步思考 Tip 3: Ask for Step-by-Step Thinking

对于复杂问题，不要让 AI 一步给出答案。要求它"先思考再回答"或者"分步骤分析"，能大幅提高输出的质量和准确性。这是因为在分步思考的过程中，AI 会自动发现和修正自己的逻辑错误。 For complex problems, don't have AI give an answer in one step. Ask it to "think first, then answer" or "analyze step by step" — this dramatically improves output quality and accuracy. This is because during step-by-step thinking, AI automatically discovers and corrects its own logical errors. 

请分步骤分析以下问题：

我们公司正在考虑是否要进入东南亚市场。请按照以下步骤进行分析：
1. 先列出东南亚市场的主要机会和风险
2. 分析我们公司目前的能力是否匹配
3. 给出你的结论和建议

请在每一步给出详细的论据。Please analyze the following problem step by step:

Our company is considering whether to enter the Southeast Asian market. Please analyze according to these steps:
1. First list the main opportunities and risks of the Southeast Asian market
2. Analyze whether our company's current capabilities are a good match
3. Give your conclusions and recommendations

Please provide detailed supporting arguments for each step.

技巧 4：用限制条件框定输出 Tip 4: Use Constraints to Frame the Output

ChatGPT 的默认行为是"多说一点、全面一点"。但很多时候，你需要的不是百科全书式的回答，而是特定格式、特定长度、特定角度的精准输出。用限制条件框定它，效果会好得多。 ChatGPT's default behavior is "say more, be more comprehensive." But often you don't need an encyclopedic answer — you need precise output in a specific format, length, and angle. Use constraints to frame it, and the results will be much better. 

请帮我写一篇关于 AI 对教育影响的文章。
要求：
- 字数：800-1000 字
- 目标读者：中学教师
- 语调：温和、鼓励、不贩卖焦虑
- 包含 3 个具体的教学场景案例
- 不要使用技术术语
- 最后一段给出鼓励性的总结Please write an article about AI's impact on education.
Requirements:
- Word count: 800-1000 words
- Target audience: middle school teachers
- Tone: warm, encouraging, no fear-mongering
- Include 3 specific teaching scenario examples
- No technical jargon
- End with an encouraging summary paragraph

技巧 5：让 AI 反问你 Tip 5: Ask AI to Ask You Questions

这是很多人忽略的一个技巧。如果你不确定自己的需求是否足够清晰，可以在提问的最后加上一句"在你回答之前，请先问我 3 个问题来更好地理解我的需求"。这样 AI 会先反问你，你回答之后它再给出更精准的结果。 This is a technique many people overlook. If you're not sure whether your request is clear enough, add "Before you answer, ask me 3 questions to better understand my needs." AI will first ask you questions, then give more precise results after you answer. 

我想要做一个个人品牌，但不太确定从哪个方向开始。
在给出建议之前，请先问我以下信息：
1. 我目前的职业和专业背景
2. 我最擅长的技能是什么
3. 我希望吸引什么样的受众
4. 我每周能投入多少时间

收到我的回答后再给出具体的个人品牌建设方案。I want to build a personal brand but I'm not sure which direction to start.
Before giving advice, please ask me for the following information:
1. My current profession and professional background
2. What my strongest skills are
3. What kind of audience I want to attract
4. How much time I can invest per week

Give me a specific personal branding plan after receiving my answers.

技巧 6：指定输出格式 Tip 6: Specify the Output Format

如果你需要特定格式的输出——表格、列表、代码、Markdown、JSON——直接告诉 ChatGPT。不要让它自己决定格式，因为你想要的可能和它默认输出的不一样。 If you need output in a specific format — table, list, code, Markdown, JSON — tell ChatGPT directly. Don't let it decide the format itself, because what you want may differ from its default output. 

请对比以下三款手机的价格、电池续航、摄像头参数、处理器型号。
请以 Markdown 表格的格式输出，第一列是参数名称，后面三列分别是对应的参数值。
按价格从低到高排列。Please compare the following three phones in terms of price, battery life, camera specs, and processor model.
Output in Markdown table format — first column is parameter name, the next three columns are corresponding values.
Order by price from low to high.

技巧 7：用"不要做什么"来排除错误方向 Tip 7: Use "Don'ts" to Eliminate Wrong Directions

有时候告诉 AI "不要做什么"比告诉它"要做什么"更有效。通过排除你不想要的内容，可以大幅减少 AI 输出中"看起来对但其实不是你想要的"部分。 Sometimes telling AI what "not to do" is more effective than telling it what "to do." By eliminating content you don't want, you can dramatically reduce the parts of the output that "look right but aren't what you want." 

请帮我写一篇产品介绍文案。
要求：
- 风格要轻松幽默，像在和朋友聊天
- 不要使用"颠覆性"、"极致"、"赋能"这类空洞的互联网黑话
- 不要超过 500 字
- 不要写技术参数，重点写使用体验
- 不要用感叹号结尾Please write a product introduction copy.
Requirements:
- Style should be light and humorous, like chatting with a friend
- Don't use empty internet buzzwords like "disruptive," "ultimate," "empower"
- Don't exceed 500 words
- Don't write technical specs, focus on user experience
- Don't end sentences with exclamation marks

技巧 8：拆分大任务为小对话 Tip 8: Break Large Tasks into Small Conversations

不要在一个对话里塞太多任务。ChatGPT 在处理单一、明确的任务时表现最好。如果你需要完成一个复杂的大项目，把它拆成多个小任务，每个任务用一个独立的对话来处理。这样每次输出质量更高，也更容易修改和调整。 Don't stuff too many tasks into one conversation. ChatGPT performs best when handling single, clear tasks. If you need to complete a complex project, break it into multiple small tasks, each handled in a separate conversation. This way each output is higher quality and easier to modify and adjust.

比如，不要在一个对话里同时让它"帮我写商业计划书、设计 Logo 方案、做市场调研"。正确的做法是：第一个对话专门做市场调研，第二个对话根据调研结果写商业计划书的核心部分，第三个对话处理 Logo 设计方向。 For example, don't ask it to "write a business plan, design a logo concept, and do market research" all in one conversation. The right approach is: first conversation for market research only, second conversation for writing the business plan's core based on research findings, third conversation for the logo design direction.

小技巧：每次新建对话时，可以在开头简要概括之前对话的关键结论，这样 ChatGPT 就能"记住"上下文。 Pro tip: When starting a new conversation, briefly summarize key conclusions from the previous conversation at the beginning, so ChatGPT can "remember" the context.

技巧 9：追问和迭代，不要一次就满意 Tip 9: Follow Up and Iterate — Don't Settle on the First Try

很少有人能在第一次提问就得到完美的输出。重要的是把 ChatGPT 当成一个"可以反复修改的草稿"——先得到一个初稿，然后通过追问和迭代不断优化。ChatGPT 的最大优势之一就是你可以在同一个对话里不断打磨答案。 Few people get perfect output on the first try. The key is to treat ChatGPT as a "revisable draft" — get a first draft, then keep optimizing through follow-ups and iterations. One of ChatGPT's biggest advantages is that you can continuously refine answers within the same conversation. 

（拿到初稿后追问）
这个方案整体不错，但第三部分的论述太抽象了，请用更具体的案例来说明。
另外，开头部分能不能更简洁一些？目前的开头有 300 字，请压缩到 100 字以内。(Follow-up after getting the first draft)
The overall plan is good, but Part 3's argument is too abstract. Please use more specific cases to illustrate.
Also, can the introduction be more concise? Currently it's 300 words — please compress it to under 100 words.

技巧 10：提供背景信息，建立上下文 Tip 10: Provide Background Information and Build Context

ChatGPT 没有你的生活经验和工作背景。你脑子里觉得"显而易见"的信息，对它来说是未知的。所以在提问之前，花 30 秒提供必要的背景信息，会让输出质量提升一大截。 ChatGPT doesn't have your life experience or work background. What seems "obvious" in your head is unknown to it. So before asking, spend 30 seconds providing necessary background information, and the output quality will jump significantly. 

背景信息：
- 我们是一家 20 人的创业公司，做 SaaS 项目管理工具
- 目标客户是中小企业，客单价 2000 元/月
- 目前有 500 个付费客户，月增长率 8%
- 竞品主要有 Notion、飞书项目、Tower

基于以上背景，请帮我设计一个客户成功（Customer Success）团队的组建方案。Background:
- We're a 20-person startup building a SaaS project management tool
- Target customers are SMBs, ARPU is 2,000 RMB/month
- Currently 500 paying customers, 8% monthly growth rate
- Main competitors: Notion, Feishu Projects, Tower

Based on the above, please design a Customer Success team building plan.

总结：好的提问 = 好的输出 Summary: Good Questions = Good Output

这 10 个技巧的本质其实很简单：你花多少心思在提问上，AI 就还你多少质量的输出。很多新手觉得 ChatGPT "不好用"，其实是因为他们的提问太随意。试一试上面这些技巧，你会发现同一个 ChatGPT，突然变得"聪明了很多"。 The essence of these 10 techniques is simple: the effort you put into your question determines the quality of AI's output. Many beginners feel ChatGPT "doesn't work well," but actually it's because their prompting is too casual. Try these techniques and you'll find the same ChatGPT suddenly seems "much smarter."

不需要一次掌握所有技巧。从最常用的几个开始——角色设定、提供示例、分步思考、限制条件——这四个用好了，就足以让你的 ChatGPT 输出质量翻倍。其他的技巧可以在使用过程中逐步学习和应用。 You don't need to master all techniques at once. Start with the most commonly used ones — role setting, providing examples, step-by-step thinking, constraints — mastering these four alone is enough to double your ChatGPT output quality. The other techniques can be gradually learned and applied as you use the tool.

最后提醒：这些技巧不只适用于 ChatGPT，也适用于国产的豆包、Kimi、通义千问等所有 AI 对话工具。好的"提示词"是跨工具的通用技能。掌握了这门技能，无论用哪个 AI 工具，你都能获得高质量的输出。 Final reminder: these techniques don't just apply to ChatGPT — they work with all AI chat tools including Doubao, Kimi, Tongyi Qianwen, and others. Good "prompting" is a universal skill that transfers across tools. Master this skill, and you'll get high-quality output regardless of which AI tool you use.
