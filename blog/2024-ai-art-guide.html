为什么 AI 绘画值得认真对待 Why AI Art Deserves Serious Attention

2024 年，AI 绘画已经不是什么新鲜事了。但正是这种不新鲜，让很多人忽略了一个事实：AI 绘画工具已经从玩具进化成了真正的生产力工具。无论是做自媒体配图、电商产品图、游戏素材、建筑设计概念图，还是纯粹的艺术创作，AI 绘画都能大幅提升你的效率。问题不再是 AI 能不能画画，而是我应该用哪个工具画。 In 2024, AI art is no longer news. But precisely this familiarity has led many people to overlook a fact: AI art tools have evolved from toys into genuine productivity tools. Whether you are creating social media graphics, e-commerce product images, game assets, architectural concept art, or pure artistic creation, AI art can dramatically boost your efficiency. The question is no longer can AI draw? but which tool should I use?

目前市面上最主流的 AI 绘画工具有三款：Midjourney、Stable Diffusion（SD）和 Flux。它们各有特色，适合不同的人群和场景。这篇文章会从画质、上手难度、价格、可控性、适用场景等多个维度进行全面对比，最后根据你的实际需求给出推荐。 The three most mainstream AI art tools on the market right now are Midjourney, Stable Diffusion (SD), and Flux. Each has its own characteristics and suits different people and scenarios. This article will comprehensively compare them across dimensions like image quality, learning curve, pricing, controllability, and applicable scenarios, then give recommendations based on your actual needs.

不同需求，不同选择 Different Needs, Different Choices

选择哪个工具，本质上取决于三个问题：你要画什么、你的技术水平如何、你愿意花多少钱。 Which tool to choose essentially depends on three questions: what to draw, your technical level, and budget.

如果你是纯新手，只是想快速出好看的图：选 Midjourney。上手最快，出图质量最高，10 美元/月完全可接受。不需要懂技术概念，会描述想要的东西就行。 If you are a complete beginner wanting nice images fast: Choose Midjourney. Fastest start, highest quality, $10/month is reasonable. No technical concepts needed.

如果你是设计师或插画师，需要精细控制：选 Stable Diffusion。学习成本高，但 ControlNet 加 LoRA 让你实现几乎任何创作需求。 If you are a designer needing fine control: Choose Stable Diffusion. Higher learning cost, but ControlNet + LoRA enables almost any creative need.

如果你需要画面中包含精确文字：选 Flux。做海报、Logo、社交媒体封面等需要文字的场景，Flux 远超其他两个。 If you need precise text in images: Choose Flux. For posters, logos, and social covers, Flux far outperforms the others.

给新手的建议Tips for Beginners

不要一上来就纠结选哪个工具。如果完全没接触过 AI 绘画，先用 Midjourney 玩一周，感受 AI 绘画能做什么、不能做什么。有了基本认知后，再根据需求决定是否深入学习 SD。另外，不要只关注工具本身——学会写好 Prompt 才是核心技能。同样一个 Midjourney，有人只能生成普通的图，有人能生成惊艳的作品，差距就在 Prompt 上。多看优秀作品的 Prompt 怎么写的，多练习，这是提升最快的路径。Do not agonize over which tool from the start. If new to AI art, play with Midjourney for a week first. Once you have a basic understanding, decide based on your needs whether to dive into SD. Also, do not just focus on the tool itself — learning to write good prompts is the core skill. With the same Midjourney, some generate ordinary images while others create stunning works. The difference is in the prompts. Study excellent prompts and practice more.
