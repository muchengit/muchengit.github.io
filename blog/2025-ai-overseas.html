AI 出海掘金：一个人做 Niche Product 赚美金 Building a Niche Product with AI and Earning in USD

2025.07.05 — 一个人用 AI 做英文市场产品，从选 Niche 到收到第一笔美元的完整路径One person using AI to build products for the English market — from niche selection to receiving your first dollar

为什么出海？ Why Go Global?

中国互联网市场竞争极其激烈，一个功能上线第二天就有 10 个竞品跟进。而英文市场（美国、欧洲、东南亚英语用户）的付费意愿和付费能力远超国内市场。同样的产品，在国内可能月入 3,000 元，在海外可能月入 3,000 美元——汇率本身就是 7 倍的放大器。 The Chinese internet market is fiercely competitive — a feature launched today will have 10 competitors tomorrow. The English-speaking market (US, Europe, Southeast Asian English users) has far higher willingness and ability to pay. The same product might earn 3,000 RMB/month domestically but 3,000 USD/month overseas — the exchange rate alone is a 7x multiplier.

更关键的是，AI 彻底消除了出海的最大障碍——语言。以前做海外产品需要找英语母语者合作，现在 AI 可以帮你写英文文案、做英文 UI、回复英文用户邮件，质量接近甚至超越普通英语母语者的水平。这意味着，一个只会中文的人，也能做出面向全球市场的英文产品。 More critically, AI has completely eliminated the biggest barrier to going global — language. Before, building overseas products required partnering with native English speakers. Now AI can help you write English copy, design English UIs, and respond to English user emails at quality approaching or exceeding average native speakers. This means even someone who only speaks Chinese can build English products for the global market.

选 Niche 的方法 How to Choose Your Niche

出海不是随便做一个产品扔到海外就有人买。你需要找到一个足够小但付费意愿强的细分市场（Niche）。好的 Niche 有三个特征：用户有明确的痛点、现有解决方案不够好、用户愿意花钱解决。 Going global isn't about randomly building something and throwing it overseas. You need to find a niche market that's small enough but with strong willingness to pay. Good niches have three characteristics: users have a clear pain point, existing solutions are inadequate, and users are willing to pay to solve it.

具体怎么找？去 Reddit 找。Reddit 是全球最大的英文社区，里面有无数个细分板块（Subreddit），每个板块就是一群有共同需求的人。找那些帖子数量多但解决方案少的话题，那就是你的机会。另外，Product Hunt 上经常出现新的 Niche 产品，看看哪些产品上线后反响好但功能还不够完善，你也可以做一个更好的版本。 How exactly? Go to Reddit. Reddit is the world's largest English community with countless subreddits — each is a group of people with shared needs. Find topics with lots of posts but few solutions — that's your opportunity. Also, Product Hunt frequently features new niche products; look for ones with good reception but incomplete features, and build a better version.

💡

实用技巧：用 AI 帮你分析 Niche。把一个 Reddit 帖子的链接丢给 ChatGPT，让它分析这个社区的核心痛点和未被满足的需求。AI 能从几百条评论中提炼出你手动看可能需要几个小时才能发现的规律。另外，关注 Twitter/X 上 indie hacker 圈子的讨论，很多 Niche 机会最早在那里出现。 Practical tip: Use AI to help analyze niches. Paste a Reddit thread link into ChatGPT and have it analyze the community's core pain points and unmet needs. AI can extract patterns from hundreds of comments that might take you hours to discover manually. Also, follow indie hacker discussions on Twitter/X — many niche opportunities appear there first.

出海五步流程 The 5-Step Go-Global Process

1

选 Niche & 验证需求 Choose Niche & Validate Demand

用上述方法选定一个 Niche 后，先做需求验证。做一个简单的 Landing Page（用 Vercel + Next.js 或者 Carrd），写清楚你要做什么产品、解决什么问题。投放 50-100 美元的 Google Ads 或者 Reddit Ads，看有多少人点击、多少人留邮箱。如果转化率高于 5%，说明需求真实存在，可以开始做产品。 After selecting a niche using the methods above, validate demand first. Create a simple landing page (with Vercel + Next.js or Carrd), clearly stating what product you'll build and what problem it solves. Run $50-$100 in Google Ads or Reddit Ads, see how many click and how many leave their email. If conversion rate exceeds 5%, demand is real — start building.

💡

省钱技巧：不用真的花钱投广告。把 Landing Page 发到对应的 Reddit 社区和 Twitter 上，如果有人主动问"什么时候上线"，说明需求是真实的。 Money-saving tip: You don't actually need to spend on ads. Post your landing page to the relevant Reddit community and Twitter — if people proactively ask "when is it launching?", demand is real.

2

用 AI 克服语言障碍 Overcome Language Barriers with AI

产品所有面向用户的文案都让 AI 写：产品名称、Slogan、功能描述、FAQ、帮助文档、邮件模板。用 Claude 或 GPT-4 写，给出具体的品牌调性和目标用户描述，让它生成地道的英文文案。UI 文案可以用 Writer.com 或 Hemingway Editor 做最后润色，确保读起来自然。不要自己写英文——非母语者写的英文一眼就能看出来，会严重影响产品的专业感。 Have AI write all user-facing copy: product name, slogan, feature descriptions, FAQ, help docs, email templates. Use Claude or GPT-4, providing specific brand tone and target user descriptions for authentic English copy. Polish UI copy with Writer.com or Hemingway Editor for natural readability. Don't write English yourself — non-native English is instantly recognizable and seriously undermines product professionalism.

💡

地道表达技巧：给 AI 一个 prompt 模板："你是一个美国本土的 SaaS 产品文案专家，帮我写一段产品介绍。目标用户是 [描述]，产品核心功能是 [描述]，品牌调性是 [专业/轻松/极客等]。用简洁、地道的美国英语，避免使用中式英语表达。" Authentic writing tip: Give AI this prompt template: "You are a US-based SaaS copywriter. Write a product introduction. Target users: [describe], core feature: [describe], brand tone: [professional/casual/geeky etc.]. Use concise, authentic American English, avoid Chinglish expressions."

3

用 AI 快速构建产品 Build the Product Fast with AI

用 Cursor 或 Windsurf 做开发，目标是 1-2 周内做出 MVP。技术栈推荐 Next.js + Supabase + Vercel，这套组合部署最简单，AI 对它的支持也最好。做产品的过程中，所有 UI 文案、错误提示、说明文字都直接用 AI 生成英文版本。不要先做中文再翻译——直接用英文开发，省去翻译环节。 Use Cursor or Windsurf for development, aiming for an MVP in 1-2 weeks. Recommended tech stack: Next.js + Supabase + Vercel — simplest deployment, best AI support. During development, have all UI copy, error messages, and help text generated directly in English by AI. Don't build in Chinese then translate — develop in English directly, skip the translation step.

4

部署到全球平台 Deploy to Global Platforms

部署到 Vercel（免费额度足够），绑定自定义域名。域名推荐用 .com 或 .io，在 Namecheap 或 Cloudflare 注册。做好 SEO：写英文的 meta description、og tags，确保 Google 能正确索引你的产品页面。然后在 Product Hunt 上发布——Product Hunt 是海外独立开发者最重要的发布渠道，一次好的发布能带来几百到几千的初始用户。 Deploy to Vercel (free tier is sufficient), bind a custom domain. Recommend .com or .io domains, register on Namecheap or Cloudflare. Do proper SEO: write English meta descriptions, og tags, ensure Google indexes your product page correctly. Then launch on Product Hunt — it's the most important launch channel for indie developers overseas, a good launch can bring hundreds to thousands of initial users.

💡

Product Hunt 发布技巧：选择周二到周四发布（避开周末），准备一个 30 秒的产品演示视频，写一个有故事性的发布描述。提前在 Twitter 上预告，当天请朋友帮忙投票。 Product Hunt launch tip: Launch Tuesday through Thursday (avoid weekends), prepare a 30-second demo video, write a story-driven launch description. Tease on Twitter in advance, ask friends to upvote on launch day.

5

设置收款 & 开始赚钱 Set Up Payments & Start Earning

收款推荐用 Stripe（需要注册美国公司或者香港公司）或者 LemonSqueezy（不需要注册公司，直接以个人身份收款）。LemonSqueezy 是对个人开发者最友好的选择——它帮你处理税务、发票、退款等所有流程，你只需要连接你的银行账户就能收到美元。定价策略：先免费或低价（$5-9/月）获取用户，有了一定用户基础后再提价。$9-29/月是独立开发者产品最常见也最合理的定价区间。 For payments, use Stripe (requires US or HK company) or LemonSqueezy (no company needed, accept payments as individual). LemonSqueezy is the most friendly option for indie developers — it handles taxes, invoices, refunds, and all processes; you just connect your bank account to receive USD. Pricing strategy: start free or low ($5-$9/month) to acquire users, raise prices after building a user base. $9-$29/month is the most common and reasonable price range for indie developer products.

常见问题 Common Questions

英语不好能做吗？完全可以。AI 可以帮你写所有英文内容，包括回复用户邮件、处理客服问题。你唯一需要做的是用中文想清楚产品逻辑，然后让 AI 帮你表达。当然，如果你能读英文（用 AI 翻译辅助也行），对了解海外用户需求会更有帮助。 Can I do this if my English isn't good? Absolutely. AI can help you write all English content, including replying to user emails and handling customer support. All you need is to think through product logic in Chinese, then let AI express it. Of course, if you can read English (AI translation helps too), understanding overseas user needs becomes easier.

需要注册海外公司吗？初期不需要。LemonSqueezy 和 Paddle 等平台允许个人开发者直接收款。当月收入稳定超过 5,000 美元时，再考虑注册香港或美国公司，使用 Stripe 获得更低的费率。 Do I need to register an overseas company? Not initially. Platforms like LemonSqueezy and Paddle allow individual developers to accept payments directly. When monthly revenue consistently exceeds $5,000, consider registering a Hong Kong or US company to use Stripe for lower fees.

收款的钱怎么回到国内？LemonSqueezy 支持直接打款到国内银行账户（以美元形式入账），或者用 Wise（原 TransferWise）做跨境转账，费率比银行低很多。另外也可以考虑用 PayPal，但费率较高不推荐作为主要收款方式。 How to get paid money back to China? LemonSqueezy supports direct wire transfer to Chinese bank accounts (in USD), or use Wise for cross-border transfers with much lower fees than banks. PayPal is also an option but with higher fees, not recommended as primary payment method.

总结：AI 让出海的门槛降到了历史最低。语言不是问题，技术不是问题，收款不是问题。唯一的问题是：你愿不愿意迈出第一步？选一个 Niche，用 AI 做一个 Landing Page，花一周时间验证需求。如果验证通过，用 Cursor 在两周内做出 MVP。如果 MVP 有用户，继续迭代。出海的核心不是"做多大的产品"，而是"找到对的小市场"。 Summary: AI has lowered the barrier to going global to a historic low. Language isn't a problem, technology isn't a problem, payments aren't a problem. The only question is: are you willing to take the first step? Pick a niche, create a landing page with AI, spend a week validating demand. If validation passes, build an MVP with Cursor in two weeks. If the MVP gets users, keep iterating. The core of going global isn't "building a big product" but "finding the right small market."
