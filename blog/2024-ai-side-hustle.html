AI 做副业的 N 种可能：2024 年普通人实操路径 AI Side Hustles: Practical Paths for Ordinary People in 2024

2024.08.12 — 7 条经过验证的 AI 副业路径，附工具推荐、收入预估和难度评估7 proven AI side hustle paths with tool recommendations, income estimates, and difficulty ratings

AI 副业的时代来了 The Era of AI Side Hustles Has Arrived

2024 年，"副业"这个词的热度空前高涨。经济环境的变化让越来越多人开始思考：如何在不辞职的情况下，用业余时间增加一份收入？AI 的爆发，恰好为这个问题提供了一个全新的答案。 In 2024, the buzz around "side hustles" reached unprecedented heights. Changes in the economic climate have more and more people thinking: how can I earn extra income in my spare time without quitting my job? The AI explosion恰好 provided a brand new answer to this question.

和传统副业不同，AI 副业有几个独特优势：启动成本低（很多 AI 工具免费或很便宜），学习周期短（不需要学几年的专业技能），时间灵活（随时可以开始和暂停），可规模化（用 AI 提高效率后，一个人能干过去几个人的活）。 Unlike traditional side hustles, AI side hustles have several unique advantages: low startup costs (many AI tools are free or cheap), short learning curve (no need to study for years to learn professional skills), flexible hours (start and pause anytime), and scalability (with AI boosting efficiency, one person can do the work that used to require several people).

"AI 不会取代你的工作，但会使用 AI 的人可能会取代不会使用 AI 的人。副业也是一样。" "AI won't replace your job, but people who use AI might replace those who don't. The same goes for side hustles."

这篇文章整理了 7 条经过实际验证的 AI 副业路径。每条路径我都会说明：做什么、需要什么工具、预期收入、难度评估。请注意，这些数字是基于市场行情的估算，实际收入因个人能力和投入时间而异。我的目的是给你提供一个"菜单"，让你根据自己的情况选择。 This article compiles 7 AI side hustle paths that have been practically verified. For each path, I'll explain: what to do, what tools you need, expected income, and difficulty rating. Note these numbers are market-based estimates — actual income varies by individual ability and time invested. My goal is to give you a "menu" so you can choose based on your own situation.

7 条 AI 副业路径 7 AI Side Hustle Paths

1

AI 写作变现 AI-Powered Writing Monetization

做什么：利用 AI 帮助你高效产出各类文字内容，然后通过平台变现。包括但不限于：公众号文章、知乎回答、小红书笔记、自媒体稿件、商业文案、产品描述等。AI 可以帮你完成初稿、润色文字、生成标题、适配不同平台的风格。 What to do: Use AI to efficiently produce various written content, then monetize through platforms. This includes but isn't limited to: WeChat official account articles, Zhihu answers, Xiaohongshu posts, self-media articles, commercial copy, product descriptions, etc. AI can help you draft, polish text, generate titles, and adapt styles for different platforms.

所需工具：ChatGPT 或豆包（写中文内容用国产工具效果更好）、Notion（内容管理）、各内容平台的编辑器。 Tools needed: ChatGPT or Doubao (Chinese tools work better for Chinese content), Notion (content management), various platform editors.

预期收入：500-5000 元/月（取决于内容质量和发布频率）。优质公众号通过流量主或接广告可以达到更高。 Expected income: 500-5,000 RMB/month (depends on content quality and posting frequency). Quality WeChat accounts can earn more through traffic ads or sponsorships.

难度评估：★☆☆☆☆（入门门槛低，但写出好内容和积累粉丝需要时间） Difficulty: ★☆☆☆☆ (low entry barrier, but writing great content and building an audience takes time)

2

AI 设计接单 AI Design Freelancing

做什么：用 AI 设计工具快速产出各类视觉设计，然后在闲鱼、淘宝、猪八戒等平台接单。常见需求包括：Logo 设计、海报设计、电商主图、社交媒体封面、PPT 美化、名片设计等。AI 设计工具的出图速度是传统设计的 10 倍以上，一个人可以同时服务多个客户。 What to do: Use AI design tools to quickly produce visual designs, then take orders on platforms like Xianyu, Taobao, Zhubajie. Common needs include: logo design, poster design, e-commerce main images, social media covers, PPT beautification, business card design, etc. AI design tools produce images 10x faster than traditional design — one person can serve multiple clients simultaneously.

所需工具：Midjourney 或 Stable Diffusion（生成图片）、Canva 或稿定设计（排版编辑）、Photoshop（精细调整，可选）。 Tools needed: Midjourney or Stable Diffusion (image generation), Canva or Gaoding Design (layout editing), Photoshop (fine-tuning, optional).

预期收入：1000-8000 元/月（单张设计 50-500 元，取决于复杂度）。 Expected income: 1,000-8,000 RMB/month (50-500 RMB per design, depending on complexity).

难度评估：★★☆☆☆（需要学习 AI 绘图的基本操作，但不需传统美术基础） Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ (need to learn basic AI image generation, but no traditional art background required)

3

AI 编程做产品 AI-Powered Product Building

做什么：用 Cursor 等 AI 编程工具开发小型产品（工具网站、浏览器插件、小程序、SaaS 工具等），然后通过付费订阅、广告、或一次性售卖变现。这是所有 AI 副业路径中天花板最高的——一个成功的产品可以带来被动收入，甚至发展成全职事业。 What to do: Use AI coding tools like Cursor to develop small products (utility websites, browser extensions, mini-programs, SaaS tools, etc.), then monetize through subscriptions, ads, or one-time sales. This is the highest-ceiling path among all AI side hustles — a successful product can generate passive income, or even evolve into a full-time career.

所需工具：Cursor（核心编程工具）、Vercel 或 GitHub Pages（免费部署）、ChatGPT（辅助设计和解决问题）。 Tools needed: Cursor (core coding tool), Vercel or GitHub Pages (free deployment), ChatGPT (auxiliary design and problem-solving).

预期收入：0-20000+ 元/月（变化极大，成功的产品可以带来持续收入，失败的则零收入）。 Expected income: 0-20,000+ RMB/month (highly variable — successful products can bring continuous income, failures earn zero).

难度评估：★★★☆☆（不需要会写代码，但需要产品思维和持续迭代的能力） Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ (no coding required, but needs product thinking and continuous iteration ability)

4

AI 视频制作 AI Video Production

做什么：用 AI 工具制作短视频内容，在抖音、B站、YouTube 等平台发布，通过广告分成、带货、知识付费变现。AI 可以帮你生成视频脚本、配音、字幕、甚至视频画面本身。一个人就能完成过去需要"编剧 + 配音 + 剪辑 + 出镜"整个团队的工作。 What to do: Use AI tools to create short video content, publish on Douyin, Bilibili, YouTube, and monetize through ad revenue, e-commerce, or knowledge products. AI can generate video scripts, voiceovers, subtitles, and even video visuals themselves. One person can do the work that used to require a whole team of "writer + voice actor + editor + on-camera talent."

所需工具：ChatGPT/Kimi（写脚本）、剪映/CapCut（剪辑）、TTS 工具如 ElevenLabs（AI 配音）、Runway 或 Pika（AI 视频片段生成）。 Tools needed: ChatGPT/Kimi (script writing), JianYing/CapCut (editing), TTS tools like ElevenLabs (AI voiceover), Runway or Pika (AI video clip generation).

预期收入：0-10000 元/月（取决于播放量和变现方式，起量需要时间）。 Expected income: 0-10,000 RMB/month (depends on views and monetization method, scaling takes time).

难度评估：★★☆☆☆（工具使用门槛不高，但做出有人看的视频需要创意和坚持） Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ (tool barrier is low, but making videos people actually watch requires creativity and persistence)

5

AI 翻译服务 AI Translation Services

做什么：利用 AI 进行高质量翻译，在翻译平台、自由职业网站上接单。适合有外语基础的普通人。AI 翻译的质量已经接近甚至超过普通翻译水平，再加上人工审校，输出质量完全可以满足大部分商业需求。常见场景包括：产品文档翻译、学术论文翻译、商务邮件翻译、网站本地化等。 What to do: Use AI for high-quality translation and take orders on translation platforms and freelance websites. Suitable for people with foreign language basics. AI translation quality now approaches or exceeds average human translation, and with human proofreading, output quality easily meets most commercial needs. Common scenarios include: product documentation, academic papers, business emails, website localization, etc.

所需工具：DeepL 或 ChatGPT（翻译）、MemoQ 或 Trados（专业翻译工具，可选）、Grammarly（英文校对）。 Tools needed: DeepL or ChatGPT (translation), MemoQ or Trados (professional translation tools, optional), Grammarly (English proofreading).

预期收入：1000-6000 元/月（千字翻译 100-300 元，取决于专业领域）。 Expected income: 1,000-6,000 RMB/month (100-300 RMB per 1000 characters, depending on field).

难度评估：★★☆☆☆（需要至少一门外语的基础知识，AI 负责大部分翻译工作） Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ (need basic knowledge of at least one foreign language, AI handles most translation work)

6

AI 教学和咨询 AI Teaching and Consulting

做什么：把自己使用 AI 的经验转化为教学内容，通过知识星球、小报童、线下工作坊、企业培训等方式变现。很多企业和个人想知道"如何用 AI 提效"，但不知道从何开始。如果你已经在日常中熟练使用了 AI 工具，你就可以教别人。 What to do: Turn your AI experience into teaching content, monetizing through knowledge communities, newsletters, offline workshops, or corporate training. Many businesses and individuals want to know "how to use AI to boost efficiency" but don't know where to start. If you're already proficient with AI tools in your daily life, you can teach others.

所需工具：你已有的 AI 使用经验、腾讯会议/Zoom（线上教学）、飞书/Notion（课件管理）。 Tools needed: Your existing AI experience, Tencent Meeting/Zoom (online teaching), Feishu/Notion (course material management).

预期收入：1000-15000 元/月（线上课程 99-999 元/人，企业培训 3000-10000 元/场）。 Expected income: 1,000-15,000 RMB/month (online courses 99-999 RMB/person, corporate training 3,000-10,000 RMB/session).

难度评估：★★★☆☆（需要有一定的表达能力和知识体系化能力） Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ (requires communication skills and ability to systematize knowledge)

7

AI 数据标注和处理 AI Data Annotation and Processing

做什么：很多 AI 公司需要大量人工标注的数据来训练模型。你可以利用 AI 辅助工具提高标注效率，在众包平台（如百度众测、京东众智、龙猫数据等）上接标注任务。AI 可以帮你预标注数据，你只需要审核和修正，效率比纯手工标注高 3-5 倍。 What to do: Many AI companies need large amounts of human-annotated data to train models. You can use AI-assisted tools to boost annotation efficiency and take annotation tasks on crowdsourcing platforms (like Baidu CrowdTest, JD Crowdsourcing, Longma Data, etc.). AI can help you pre-annotate data — you just review and correct it, making efficiency 3-5x higher than pure manual annotation.

所需工具：平台自带的标注工具、ChatGPT（辅助理解标注规则和复杂案例）。 Tools needed: Platform's built-in annotation tools, ChatGPT (to help understand annotation rules and complex cases).

预期收入：1000-4000 元/月（按件计费，单价取决于标注难度）。 Expected income: 1,000-4,000 RMB/month (piece-rate pay, unit price depends on annotation difficulty).

难度评估：★☆☆☆☆（门槛最低，但收入天花板也低，适合入门练手） Difficulty: ★☆☆☆☆ (lowest barrier, but also lowest income ceiling, good for beginners to practice)

如何选择适合你的路径？ How to Choose the Right Path for You?

面对这 7 条路径，不要贪多。选择的标准应该是：你的优势 + 你的兴趣 + 你能投入的时间。如果你文笔好，从 AI 写作开始；如果你有审美，从 AI 设计开始；如果你有产品思维，从 AI 编程开始；如果你外语好，从 AI 翻译开始。 Don't be greedy with these 7 paths. Your selection criteria should be: your strengths + your interests + the time you can invest. If you write well, start with AI writing; if you have an eye for aesthetics, start with AI design; if you have product thinking, start with AI programming; if you're good at foreign languages, start with AI translation.

新手建议 Advice for Beginners

如果你完全不知道从哪开始，推荐从 AI 写作 或 AI 数据标注 入手。这两个方向门槛最低，不需要任何专业基础，可以先在 1-2 周内跑通从"学习"到"赚到第一块钱"的全流程。有了第一次成功经验后，再逐步探索其他方向。 If you have no idea where to start, I recommend beginning with AI writing or AI data annotation. These two have the lowest barrier and require no professional background — you can go from "learning" to "earning your first yuan" in 1-2 weeks. After that first success, gradually explore other directions.

避坑提醒 Pitfall Warning

警惕任何承诺"轻松月入过万"的 AI 副业课程。AI 是工具，不是魔法。所有正经的副业都需要时间、精力和持续学习的投入。如果一个课程告诉你"每天 30 分钟月入 5 万"，那大概率是割韭菜。设定合理的预期，从小做起，逐步放大，才是可持续的副业路径。 Be wary of any AI side hustle course that promises "easy 10k/month income." AI is a tool, not magic. All legitimate side hustles require time, effort, and continuous learning. If a course tells you "30 minutes a day for 50k/month," it's probably a scam. Set reasonable expectations, start small, and scale gradually — that's the sustainable path.

我的建议：先行动，再完美 My Advice: Act First, Perfect Later

很多人在做副业的时候会陷入一个误区：花太多时间在"准备"上——学这个工具、学那个技巧、看无数教程……但迟迟不开始行动。结果是：准备了几个月，一分钱都没赚到。 Many people fall into a trap when starting side hustles: spending too much time "preparing" — learning this tool, that technique, watching countless tutorials... but never actually starting. Result: months of preparation, zero income earned.

正确的做法是：选定一条路径，花一两天学习最基础的工具操作，然后立刻开始做。在做的过程中遇到问题再解决，需要什么技能再补充什么技能。AI 工具迭代很快，你现在学的操作方式可能下个月就变了。与其花时间"准备完美"，不如在实战中快速迭代。 The right approach: pick one path, spend 1-2 days learning the most basic tool operations, then start immediately. Solve problems as you encounter them, learn skills as you need them. AI tools iterate fast — what you learn now might change next month. Instead of spending time on "perfect preparation," iterate quickly in practice.

2024 年是 AI 副业的黄金窗口期。工具成熟了，市场接受了，但大多数人还没有行动起来。这意味着竞争还不激烈，机会还很多。不要等到"准备好了"才开始——你的竞争对手可能已经在用 AI 赚钱了。选一条路径，今天就开始。 2024 is the golden window for AI side hustles. Tools are mature, the market has accepted them, but most people haven't acted yet. This means competition isn't fierce and opportunities are plentiful. Don't wait until you're "ready" to start — your competitors might already be earning money with AI. Pick a path and start today.
